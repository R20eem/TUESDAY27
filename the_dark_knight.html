About The Dark Knight

"The Dark Knight" is a 2008 superhero film directed, co-produced, and co-written by Christopher Nolan. Featuring Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne/Batman, the film follows Batman's efforts to combat crime and corruption in Gotham City, culminating in a psychological battle with his arch-nemesis, the Joker, portrayed by Heath Ledger in an Oscar-winning performance.
