About Inception

"Inception" is a 2010 science fiction film written and directed by Christopher Nolan. It explores the concept of "dream within a dream," where skilled thieves enter people's subconscious to steal or plant ideas. The film is known for its complex narrative, innovative special effects, and exploration of the nature of reality and dreams.
